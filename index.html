Textile Design Program

Salite offers a 3 Year Textile Design Program throughout which students are taught the fundamentals of design and their functionality at every phase of designing, sampling, production, marketing, and merchandising.

Why Choose Salite?

Here at Salite's Textile Program we offer:

  • A Degree upon completion of the 3 Year Program
  • Mandatory 30 hour work placement
  • Industry-based training
  • Internship opportunities during the study period
